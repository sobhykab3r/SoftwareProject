Popular Movies

Life of Pi

Life of Pi is a novel by Yann Martel that tells the story of Pi Patel, a young Indian boy who finds himself stranded in the Pacific Ocean with a Bengal tiger as his only companion.

Oddity

“Oddity” is a 2024 Irish horror film written and directed by Damian McCarthy. The story follows Dani Odello-Timmis, a blind medium and curio shopkeeper, who is still grieving the death of her twin sister a year prior.

Venom:The Last Dance

In the third installment of the Venom trilogy. The movie follows Eddie and Venom as they navigate their complicated relationship, with the duo facing pursuit from both human and alien forces.
